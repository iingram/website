A Whiskerer (2019-ongoing)

A whiskerer Rats feel the world with their faces. Thomas Nagle underscored how different from humans are bats who sense the shape of the world with sound. Rats hug the earth and the sides of things and so are always closer to other objects than bats but their perception of the shape of the world through their whiskers is again so .
